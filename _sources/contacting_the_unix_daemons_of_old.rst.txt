=========================================================
Contacting the UNIX Daemons of Old
=========================================================

Daemons are a long-running background process that answers requests for services. In Windows these are typically referred to as Services and are a little bit more complicated.

While we won't be going into depth today about daemons, because it tends to be a narrow and twisty rabbit hole I will be covering the basics of creating your own UNIX-like daemons.
